Yorkshire and Durham Geometry Day
at Leeds

Friday 20 January 2006



  • 10.30 - Coffee outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 11.00 - Karel Packalen (Kalmar)
    On singular connections and their applications

  • 13.15 - Luis Fernandez (Bath and Bogota)
    On the space of harmonic 2-spheres in the n-sphere

  • 14.30 - John Bolton (Durham)
    Transforms of minimal surfaces in the 5-sphere

  • 15.30 - Tea outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 16.00 - Shaun Bullett (Queen Mary)
    Mating and pinching in holomorphic dynamics (abstract)

All interested are welcome to attend. All lectures in classroom G, level 8, School of Mathematics.

Dinner will be organized at a local restaurant.

Please let Martin Speight (speight@maths.leeds.ac.uk) know if you intend to arrive by car.
