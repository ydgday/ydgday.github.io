Yorkshire Differential Geometry Day

A joint seminar on differential geometry by the Universities of Hull, Leeds and York

University of Hull, 2 November 2001

PROGRAMME


10:30 - 11:15 Coffee
11:15 - 12:15 Josef Dorfmeister (Munich): Harmonic maps and loop groups
12:15 - 13:30 Lunch
13:30 - 14:30 Andreas Arvanitoyeorgos (Athens): Riemannian flag manifolds with homogeneous geodesics
14:45 - 15:45 John Rawnsley (Warwick): Symplectic connections with curvature of Ricci type
15:45 - 16:30 Tea
16:30 - 17:30 Dominic Joyce (Oxford): Special Lagrangian 3-folds and integrable systems
18:30 - Dinner


The lectures will be in Wilberforce Building, Seminar Room 25.
Coffee and Tea will be served in Loten Building, Room 807.
