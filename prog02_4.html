Yorkshire Differential Geometry Day
at Leeds

Monday 23 June 2003



  • 10.30 - Coffee outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 11.00 - Ian Strachan (Hull)
    Genus 1 Gromov-Witten invariants for Hurwitz spaces

  • 13.15 - David Auckly (Kansas State)
    Topology, analysis and the Skyrme model

  • 14.30 - Paul Sutcliffe (Canterbury)
    Domain wall geometry

  • 15.30 - Tea outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 16.00 - John McKay (Concordia, Montreal)
    Replicable functions, ordered lattices, dispersionless KP flow, and Witten's manifold

All interested are welcome to attend. All lectures in Mathematics, Room J, level 8.

Dinner will be organized at a local restaurant.

Please let John C. Wood (j.c.wood@leeds.ac.uk) know if you intend to arrive by car.
For information on the location of the University and a Campus Map, please consult the web page: http://tldynamic.leeds.ac.uk/leisure/alphabet.asp

Yorkshire Differential Geometry Days are supported by a Scheme 3 grant from the London Mathematical Society. More information on the series is available at:
http://www.amsta.leeds.ac.uk/pure/geometry/ydgd/index.html
