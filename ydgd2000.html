Yorkshire Differential Geometry Days in 2000/01
  • YDGD0 University of Hull, 31/3/00

  • YDGD1 University of York, 29/11/00

  • YDGD2 University of Leeds, 23/3/01

  • YDGD3 University of Hull, 18-19/5/01

  • YDGD4 University of York, 5/9/01

There were five Yorkshire Differential Geometry Days during the 2000/01 academic year, organized by geometers at the Universities of Hull, Leeds and York. The local organizers were:

Jürgen Berndt, University of Hull

John C Wood, University of Leeds

Ian McIntosh & Chris Wood, University of York

The Yorkshire Differential Geometry Days were supported by a Scheme 3 grant from the London Mathematical Society. This financial support has been renewed for the academic year 2001/2002. Click here for the current programme.
