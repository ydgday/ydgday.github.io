Yorkshire and Durham Geometry Day
at Leeds

Friday 9 June 2006



  • 10.30 - Coffee outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 11.00 - Martin Svensson (Leeds)
    Harmonic morphisms from symmetric spaces

  • 12.15 - Lunch break (Senior Common Room)

  • 13.15 - Norbert Peyerimhoff (Durham)
    The Pompeiu problem on Damek-Ricci spaces

  • 14.30 - Nigel Hitchin (Oxford)
    Poisson surfaces and bihermitian metrics

  • 15.30 - Tea outside Mathematics common room, level 9

  • 16.00 - Maciej Dunajski (Cambridge)
    Topology and dynamics of time dependent unitons

All interested are welcome to attend. All lectures in classroom H, level 8, School of Mathematics.

Dinner will be organized at a local restaurant.

Please let Martin Speight (speight@maths.leeds.ac.uk) know if you intend to arrive by car.
For information on the location of the University and a Campus Map, please consult the web page: http://tldynamic.leeds.ac.uk/campusmap/alphabet.asp
