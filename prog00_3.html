Workshop on

GEOMETRY AND INTEGRABLE SYSTEMS
University of Hull, 18-19 May 2001




Friday, 18 May
14:00 - 15:00 Sergej Natanzon (Moscow): Integrable systems and effectivisation of Riemann's theorem about holomorphic maps
15:00 - 15:30 Tea
15:30 - 16:30 Jose Figueroa-O'Farrill (Edinburgh): Recent geometrical results in supergravity
16:45 - 17:45 Janos Szenthe (Budapest): On the existence of homogeneous geodesics, and applications
19:00 - Dinner
Saturday, 19 May
9:15 - 10:15 Sergei Agafonov (Loughborough): Equations of associativity, Veronese variety and projective theory of congruences
10:15 - 10:45 Coffee
10:45 - 11:45 Nicholas Woodhouse (Oxford): Twistors and isomonodromy
12:00 - 13:00 Gary Gibbons (Cambridge): New developments in cohomogeneity one metrics of special holonomy


The lectures will be in Loten Building, Lecture Room B.
Coffee and Tea will be served in Loten Building, Room 807.
