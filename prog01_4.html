Yorkshire Differential Geometry Day

A joint seminar on differential geometry by the Universities of Hull, Leeds and York

University of Hull, 14 June 2002

PROVISIONAL PROGRAMME


10:30 - 11:15 Coffee
11:15 - 12:15 Alberto Pambira (Leeds): On harmonic maps and morphisms between degenerate semi-Riemannian manifolds
12:15 - 13:30 Lunch
13:30 - 14:30 Antonio Di Scala (Hull): Normal holonomy of submanifolds and related topics
14:45 - 15:45 Bennett Palmer (Durham): Stability of minimal lagrangian submanifolds
15:45 - 16:30 Tea
16:30 - 17:30 Yann Rollin (Edinburgh): Einstein rigidity of the complex hyperbolic plane
18:30 - Dinner


The lectures will be in Loten Building, Lecture Room B.
Coffee and Tea will be served in Loten Building, Room 807.
